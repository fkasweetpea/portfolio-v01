hello!

i'm elise raich-chapman, a communications design major at carnegie mellon university, minoring in hci (human-computer interaction) and physical computing.

i believe that our physical interactions affect every other interaction we take, including how we interact with 2D space. my work is made to be existed with, not just observed. i create to explore.
